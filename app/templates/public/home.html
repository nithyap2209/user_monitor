Features

Powerful Analysis Tools

Everything you need to monitor, analyze, and respond to your social media audience — in one platform.

Unified Comment Inbox

View and respond to comments from all connected platforms in one unified feed. Filter by sentiment, platform, or status.

AI Sentiment Analysis

Multi-engine NLP ensemble using VADER, TextBlob, and regional keyword analysis. Supports Tanglish, Hinglish, and 50+ languages.

Multi-Language Translation

Auto-detect and translate comments from any language to English. Handles Tanglish, Hinglish, and mixed-script text.

Direct Reply to Comments

Reply to YouTube and Facebook comments directly from the dashboard. OAuth integration posts replies to the actual platform.

Analytics Dashboard

Visual charts for sentiment trends, platform breakdown, comment volume over time, and engagement metrics at a glance.

Auto Lead Extraction

Automatically detect and extract phone numbers and email addresses from comments. Build your contact list from social interactions.
