Cookie Policy

How Fourth Dimension User Monitor uses cookies and similar technologies.

This Cookie Policy explains how Fourth Dimension User Monitor uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website or use our platform. It outlines what these technologies are, why we use them, and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow a site to recognize your device, remember your preferences, and help improve your user experience. Cookies may be set by us (first-party cookies) or by third-party services we integrate with (third-party cookies).

Why We Use Cookies

Fourth Dimension User Monitor uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Access: To allow you to log in and securely use the dashboard and account areas
  • Session Management: To manage your current session and track basic user interactions like scan history and navigation
  • Performance & Analytics: To understand how the platform is used and improve product performance
  • User Preferences: To remember UI configurations like theme settings for a smoother experience

Privacy First

We do not use cookies for advertising or behavioral tracking. Our cookie usage is focused on functionality and product improvement.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Strictly Necessary

Required for core platform functions such as login and secure browsing.

session_id Maintains your login session and authentication state
csrf_token Protects against CSRF attacks on form submissions and API calls

Functional Functional

Analytics Analytics

Help us understand how users interact with features and flows.

Cookie Purpose
_ga Google Analytics — distinguishes new and returning users
_gid Google Analytics — tracks user interactions

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services to help us monitor system performance or gather anonymized usage insights. These third-party services may set their own cookies in your browser. For example:

  • Google Analytics may store a cookie to differentiate new and returning users
  • Cloudflare or CDN providers may use cookies to optimize security or traffic routing

Fourth Dimension User Monitor does not allow third-party advertisers or social media trackers to place cookies on our platform.

How You Can Control Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Most modern browsers provide settings to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Clear existing cookies
  • Set exceptions for trusted websites

Important Note: Blocking essential cookies may affect your ability to use some features of the Fourth Dimension User Monitor platform, such as logging in or saving settings.

Consent and Updates

By continuing to use Fourth Dimension User Monitor, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You may withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or clearing cookies.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements, third-party services, or platform features. When we do, the "Last Updated" date will be revised.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page and updating the effective date.
