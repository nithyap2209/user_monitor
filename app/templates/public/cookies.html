Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to the site owners.

2. Cookies We Use

Cookie Purpose Duration Type
session Maintains your login session and stores authentication state. Browser session Essential
csrf_token Protects against Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks on form submissions. Browser session Essential
remember_token Keeps you logged in across browser sessions ("Remember Me" feature). 30 days Functional

3. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function. They enable core features like user authentication and security. Without these cookies, the Service cannot operate. They do not collect personal information for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Cookies

User Monitor does not use third-party tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies. We do not embed third-party trackers or ad networks. The only external resources loaded are:

  • Tailwind CSS CDN — For styling (does not set cookies).
  • Font Awesome CDN — For icons (does not set cookies).
  • Chart.js CDN — For dashboard charts (does not set cookies).

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually.
  • Block third-party cookies.
  • Block cookies from specific sites.
  • Block all cookies.
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser.

Please note that if you block essential cookies, you will not be able to log in or use the Service.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us through your account administrator.
