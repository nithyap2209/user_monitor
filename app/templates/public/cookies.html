Cookie Policy

Effective Date: March 2026 · Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how User Monitor uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and platform. It describes what cookies are, why we use them, and how you can control them.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They enable the site to recognize your device, remember preferences, and enhance your user experience.

There are two main types:

  • First-party cookies: Set by User Monitor directly to provide our service
  • Third-party cookies: Set by integrated services (e.g., analytics tools) that operate on our platform

3. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Access: Enabling secure login and dashboard functionality
  • Session Management: Tracking your current session and user interactions
  • Security: Protecting against Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) attacks
  • User Preferences: Remembering your UI configurations and settings

User Monitor does not use cookies for advertising, behavioral tracking, or marketing purposes.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for the Service to function. Without them, you cannot log in or use the platform.

Cookie Purpose Duration
session Maintains your login session and authentication state Browser session
csrf_token Protects against CSRF attacks on form submissions and API calls Browser session

Functional Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and enhance your experience.

Cookie Purpose Duration
remember_token Keeps you logged in across browser sessions ("Remember Me" feature) 30 days
locale_pref Remembers your preferred language and UI settings 1 year

5. Third-Party Cookies

Our platform loads the following external resources which do not set tracking cookies:

  • Tailwind CSS CDN — For styling (no cookies)
  • Font Awesome CDN — For icons (no cookies)
  • Chart.js CDN — For dashboard charts (no cookies)

User Monitor does not allow third-party advertisers or social media trackers to place cookies on our platform.

6. How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Note: If you block essential cookies (session, csrf_token), you will not be able to log in or use User Monitor. These cookies are required for the platform to function.

7. Consent and Withdrawal

By continuing to use User Monitor, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time by:

  • Adjusting your browser's cookie settings
  • Clearing existing cookies from your browser
  • Discontinuing use of the platform
