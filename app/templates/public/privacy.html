Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026 · Last Updated: March 2026

Our Privacy Principles

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data to anyone.
  • We only collect information essential to provide our service.
  • You retain full control over your data, including the right to delete it.
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

1. Scope and Purpose

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the User Monitor platform and website. By accessing or using our service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the platform constitutes agreement with these practices.

2. Information We Collect

Account Data

  • Name, email address, and encrypted passwords
  • Company name (optional)
  • Role and designation within your organization

Social Media Data

  • Comments, posts, author names, and engagement metrics fetched from your connected social media accounts (YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter) via their official APIs
  • OAuth tokens and API keys you provide to connect your social media accounts
  • Sync timestamps and comment counts

Usage and Technical Data

  • Browser type, IP address, operating system, and device type
  • Pages visited, features used, and actions taken within the dashboard
  • Comment analysis results (sentiment scores, translations)

Cookies and Session Data

We use cookies for secure login, session tracking, and CSRF protection. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the User Monitor service
  • To fetch, display, and analyze comments from your connected social media pages
  • To perform AI-powered sentiment analysis and language translations on comments
  • To enable you to reply to comments directly from the dashboard
  • To automatically detect and extract contact information (leads) from comments
  • To send service-related communications (email verification, password resets)
  • To maintain security, prevent fraud, and ensure platform stability
  • To comply with legal obligations

Your data is not used for third-party marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We may share data only with:

  • Social Media Platforms: When you reply to comments or perform actions, data is sent to the respective platform (YouTube, Facebook, etc.) via their official API.
  • Translation Services: Comment text may be sent to Google Translate for translation purposes.
  • AI Services: Comment text may be processed by AI services (e.g., OpenAI GPT) for sentiment analysis, if configured by your administrator.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Internal Authorized Personnel: Team members with appropriate role-based access within your organization.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • Synced comments, posts, and analysis data are removed within 60 days
  • API credentials and OAuth tokens are deleted immediately
  • System logs may be retained as required by law

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting your administrator.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure cloud infrastructure hosting
  • Encrypted password storage (hashed with salt)
  • CSRF protection on all form submissions and API calls
  • Role-based access controls and permission management
  • OAuth tokens stored securely in the database

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Disconnect social media accounts at any time
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing
  • Opt out of non-critical communications
  • Export your data

Requests will be addressed within 7–10 working days.

8. International Operations

User Monitor operates from India. Your data is processed and stored in India under applicable Indian data protection laws. By using the service, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in India.

9. Children's Privacy

User Monitor is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover that data has been collected from a user under 18, it will be deleted immediately.

10. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites (social media platforms, Google services, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or platform notifications. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Grievance Officer

In accordance with IT Rules 2021, a Grievance Officer is available to address any concerns regarding data privacy. The officer is reachable Monday–Friday, 10:00 AM–6:00 PM (IST) via the contact information below.
