Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use User Monitor:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Social Media Data: Comments, posts, author names, and engagement metrics fetched from your connected social media accounts (YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter) via their official APIs.
  • API Credentials: OAuth tokens and API keys you provide to connect your social media accounts. These are stored securely and used solely for API access.
  • Usage Data: Log data such as pages visited, features used, and actions taken within the dashboard.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the User Monitor service.
  • To fetch, display, and analyze comments from your connected social media pages.
  • To perform sentiment analysis and translations on comments.
  • To enable you to reply to comments directly from the dashboard.
  • To send you service-related notifications (e.g., email verification).
  • To improve our service and fix bugs.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Social Media Platforms: When you reply to comments or perform actions, data is sent to the respective platform via their API.
  • Translation Services: Comment text may be sent to Google Translate for translation purposes.
  • AI/NLP Services: Comment text may be processed by AI services (e.g., OpenAI GPT) for sentiment analysis, if configured.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely. API keys and OAuth tokens are stored in our database. We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. Access to the application is protected by authentication and role-based permissions.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Social media comments synced to our system are retained until you delete them or disconnect the page.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Disconnect social media accounts at any time.
  • Request export of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.

7. Cookies

We use cookies for session management and authentication. See our Cookie Policy for more details.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email address associated with your account administrator.
